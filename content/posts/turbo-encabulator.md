### Key Components
- **Base Plate:** Pre-famulated amulite
- **Casing:** Malleable logarithmic
- **Bearings:** Spurving
- **Fan:** Panametric
- **Marzlevanes:** Hydrocoptic (6 units)
- **Wiring:** Lotus-o-delta in panendermic semi-boloid slots
- **Differential Girdle Spring:** Connects to grammeters
